Welcome to "Analyze Calendar".

This application fetches events from your Google Calendar and shows you some quick stats on how much of your time is booked. It was developed as a "hackweek project" from Chartbeat. Note that you need to authorize with Google twice: Once for logging into this application, and once for allowing the application to read your calendar data.

Start analyzing your calendar

Beware: This is all very alpha, and there is a lot of things it doesn't handle. A few being:

  • doesn't handle multi-day events
  • completely unware of time zones
  • does not know about holidays
  • (and many more)
